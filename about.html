    India’s Most Reliable Career Discovery Platform

    MINDWRKS started its operations in the year 2005 as CMC(Candid Management Consultants got renamed as MINDWRKS in 2023). The company is fundamentally an institution of In-depth knowledge; Expertise and Intelligence, build of a CONVOY of experts, consultants & trainers who have worked in different domains and industrial segment and different Geographies, their experience ranges from 18 years to 30 years. Therefore, we provide cohort concept platform for our clients which solely aims to provide need-based consultation to clients through intellectuals and expert minds addressing the business & people needs. The company is promoted by qualified professionals having strong background of People Science have worked in domestic market and international market. To ensure quality in Service Delivery MINDWRKS has a strong process of assessment before it tie-ups with consultants, trainers and field experts.
